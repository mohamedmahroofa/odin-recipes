Summer Sweet Smoothies

Packed full of sweet summer fruity goodness. Great for a breakfast or lunch when you're on the go.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cranberry Juice
  • 2 Cups Strawberry
  • 1 cup Blueberry
  • 1 cup Watermleon chunks
  • 1 Banana
  • 2 Fresh figs

Steps

  1. Process the cranberry juice, strawberries, blueberries, watermelon, banana, and figs in a blender until smooth and creamy.
  2. Enjoy immediately or keep cool in refrigerator.
